About Us

Cleaning Done
the Right Way

Smalls Cleaning Services is a Polokwane-based professional cleaning company registered as High Precision Car Wash Hygiene and Cleaning Specialist. We've been transforming homes, offices, and vehicles across the Limpopo region with a hands-on, detail-driven approach.

Based at 74A Bok Street, Polokwane, we serve clients across the city and surrounding areas. Whether it's a deep sofa clean after a long winter or a full post-construction site handover — we show up prepared, professional, and ready.
